# Kevin's Business Numbers

A business clarity tool for Kevin's carpentry and sauna business. Helps work out what to charge by factoring in overhead, material costs, labour, subcontractors, contingency, profit, and GST.

## What it does

- **Overview** — Your numbers at a glance: annual overhead, billable hours, true minimum rate
- **Job Costing** — Price a job properly: materials + labour + overhead + profit + GST
- **Overhead** — Track annual fixed costs and calculate your true hourly rate
- **Materials** — Price list database with markups for timber, hardware, sauna, tiling
- **History** — All your past job costings in one place

Preloaded with Kevin's actual business costs, material prices from Bowens, and job templates for saunas, framing, tiling, and renos.

## How to use

Open `kevin/index.html` in any browser. Works offline. Data saves to your browser — nothing leaves your computer.
